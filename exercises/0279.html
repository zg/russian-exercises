Начало 1, Lesson 3, Part 3. How would you do the following? (#0279)

Directions: Print the exercise and hand it to an instructor to be checked and graded.
  1. Ask your friend if he/she knows his/her neighbors.
    .
  2. Complement someone on their room or apartment.
    .
  3. Describe at least two furniture items in your room or apartment.
    .
  4. Complement someone on how well he/she plays.
    .
  5. Tell your friend that he/she is strange.
    .
  6. Ask the secratary if he/she knows where your instructor is.
    .
  7. Your friends want to visit you. Ask them if they know where you live.
    .
