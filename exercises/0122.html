Начало 1, Lesson 4, Parts 3-4. How would you do the following? (0122)

Directions: Practice saying your responses out loud in Russian. Pay special attention to the accent. Check your grammar carefully. Reply with complete sentences. You may print or record your responses and hand the exercise to an instructor to be checked for accuracy.
  1. Tell someone that you never read a newspaper.
    .
  2. Complement someone on a well done job.
    .
  3. Tell someone that your father is good with his hands.
    .
  4. Tell someone that your mother works at the post office.
    .
  5. Tell someone your nationality.
    .
  6. Tell someone that your brother is at a concert.
    .
  7. Tell someone what you like to talk about.
    .
  8. Tell someone that right now you are not doing anything.
    .
  9. Ask someone what he/she likes to talk about.
    .
  10. Tell someone that you don't understand anything in Italian.
    .
  11. Respond to a complement about how well you speak Russian.
    .
  12. Ask for permission to smoke.
    .
  13. You ask someone if Lena Sergeevna has a brother.
    .
  14. You ask someone if Anton Pavlovich has a new car.
    .
  15. Tell someone that Jim and Nina don't have a dog.
    .
  16. Ask someone for permission to play here.
    .
  17. Tell someone you are pleased to meet them.
    .
  18. Someone is calling you. Tell them you'll be right there.
    .
  19. Ask someone what they like like to do.
    .
  20. Someone asks you if you like classical music. How would you reply?
