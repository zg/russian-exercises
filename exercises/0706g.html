Голоса 1, Lesson 6. Essay. (0706g)

﻿Directions:
Pretend you have a nice two-story house and a nice new car. Describe which rooms are on the first floor (at least two), and which are on the second floor. Indicate whose is each of at least 2 bedrooms. Describe at least any two rooms in detail, indicating the color of the room, what objects (at least three) are found in each room, the color of each object and its location in the room (use verbs of location). Describe your car using at least two adjectives (one should be the color) and where it is located (garage or street).
