Начало 1, Lesson 5, Part 1. Questions. (0607)

Directions: Learn how to ask and answer these and similar questions. Practice asking and answering the questions out loud. You may send your written and oral answers to the instructor to be checked for accuracy. Record and send!
  1. What's your major?
  2. Where do you park the car?
  3. In which city do you want to live?
  4. What do you want to do tonight?
  5. Do you study at a large or small university?
  6. On which floor do you live?
  7. In what kind of city do your parents live?
  8. Do you want to play basketball?
  9. Who wants coffee?
  10. Where are your books?
  11. Where does your mother work?
