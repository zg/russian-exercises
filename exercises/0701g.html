Голоса 1, Lesson 6. Translation. (0701g)

Directions: Translate these sentences into good Russian.
  1. I have a small apartment.
  2. Does your sister have a computer?
  3. Our father has a new car.
  4. This student (male) has Russian books.
  5. Does your (polite) brother have a new cellphone?
  6. Their dog has a small house.
  7. We have a new table.
  8. The have a large refrigerator.
  9. The Anerican president has a beautiful wife.
  10. Our family has an old dacha.
