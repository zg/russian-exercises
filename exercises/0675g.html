Голоса 1, Lesson 3. How would you do the following? (0675g)

Directions: Practice saying your responses out loud in Russian. Pay special attention to the accent. Check your grammar carefully. Reply with complete sentences. You may print or record your responses and hand the exercise to an instructor to be checked for accuracy.
  1. You want to ask someone (formally) if they know [language].
  2. Tell someone how well you read, write and speak [language].
  3. Complement someone on how well they speak [language].
  4. Reply to a complement on how well you speak [language].
  5. Ask someone if his/her parents are [nationality].
  6. Ask someone in what language they speak at home.
  7. Ask someone where their parents live.
  8. Ask someone what nationality they are.
  9. Describe how well you can speak, read and understand a language.
  10. Tell in what kind of house or apartment you live.
  11. You want to find out where your friend studied [language].
  12. You want to ask your friend in which states and cities he/she has lived.
  13. Tell someone in what kind of town you live.
  14. You overhear a conversation and you want to find out what the people are talking about.
  15. You overhear a conversation and you want to find out whom the people are talking about.
