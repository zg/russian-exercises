Начало 1, Lesson 4, Part 1. How would you do the following? (#0097)

Directions: Practice saying your responses out loud in Russian. Pay special attention to the accent. Check your grammar carefully. Reply with complete sentences. You may print or record your responses and hand the exercise to an instructor to be checked for accuracy.
  1. Tell someone you have a terrible problem.
    .
  2. Ask someone what kind of a problem they have.
    .
  3. Ask your class who has your book.
    .
  4. Tell someone that they have a large apartment.
    .
  5. Tell someone what three things you don't have.
    .
  6. Tell your new landlord that your apartment is horrible.
    .
  7. Ask someone what kind of a television they have.
