Начало 1, Lesson 3, Part 1. Dialogue templates. (0272)

Directions: Prepare a dialogue according to each template. Practice saying the dialogues without looking at a Russian text. Be prepared to perform the dialogues with a partner.
Dialogue 1
A. Name, what are you doing?
B. work|listen to radio|read a letter|read a magazine|write a letter.
A. And what is Name doing?
B. work|listen to radio|read a letter|read a magazine|write a letter.

Dialogue 2
A. Where do you|your parents|your friend live?
B. I|he|she|they live in town.
A. And what kind of town is that?
B. It is a adjective town.
