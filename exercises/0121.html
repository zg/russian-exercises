Начало 1, Lesson 4, Part 2. How would you do the following? (0121)

Directions: You may print the exercise and hand it to an instructor to be checked for accuracy.
  1. Tell someone that he/she shouldn't be playing very loudly.
    .
  2. Tell someone that you like only rock music.
    .
  3. Ask someone if he/she has nice neighbors
    .
  4. Ask someone if he/she speaks Spanish.
    .
  5. Someone is looking out the window. Ask them what they see outside.
    .
  6. Tell someone what sport you like to play.
    .
  7. Tell someone what instrument you like to play.
    .
  8. Someone wants to know if you know a foreign language. Tell them that you speak only English.
    .
  9. Ask you teacher what's 'elevator' in Russian.
    .
