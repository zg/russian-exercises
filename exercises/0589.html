Начало 1, Lesson 5, Part 2. Dialogue templates. (0589)

Directions: Prepare a dialogue according to each template. Practice saying the dialogues without looking at a Russian text. Be prepared to perform the dialogues with a partner.
Dialogue АА
A. --Name, where were you yesterday?
B. --I went to place.
A. --Do you like place?
B. --Yes, I like it very much.

Dialogue ББ
A. --Do you happen to know where place is?
B. --Yes, it's downtown.
A. --Thank you.
