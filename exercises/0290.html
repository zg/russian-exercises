Начало 1, Lesson 5, Part 1. УНИВЕРСИТЕТ (#0290)

In this assignement you will access the home page of a university in Russia and find out as much information about the place as you can without using a dictionary.
  1. Visit the home page of this university. (Not yet available!)
  2. What is the name of the University?
  3. Look at the top left side of the page and find the link to the academic disciplines available at this university.
  4. List as many disciplines as you can recognize.
  5. Click on the links to several disciplines and list as many department names as you can recognize.
  6. What other pertinent information can you gather, without using a dictionary, about this university?
