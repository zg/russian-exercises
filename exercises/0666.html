Голоса 1, Урок 2. Vocabulary Puzzle with Audio Clues. (0666g)

Directions: Click on a number on the right to hear a clue. Type the appropriate word in the box. A wrong letter will appear in red. You can also type a letter by clicking on it below the grid. The Russian letter "Я" cannot be clicked on some systems.
You need Java enabled to view the crossword applet.

If you do not have Java installed you can obtain it from java.com. If do have Java you may need to check your security settings to make sure that applets are enabled, especially if you are viewing the puzzle from your hard disk. In Windows XP you may be able to enable the applet by clicking on the yellow bar at the top of the window and selecting "Allow blocked content". 		Across
