Начало 1, Lesson 7, Part 1. How would you do the following? (#0170)

Directions: Practice saying your responses out loud in Russian. Pay special attention to the accent. Check your grammar carefully. Reply with complete sentences. You may print or record your responses and hand the exercise to an instructor to be checked for accuracy.
  1. Tell your dad you want to invite your friend X on Saturday.
    .
  2. Ask a friend what he/she will be doing on Sunday.
    .
  3. Tell someone that, at the moment you are learning Russian words.
    .
  4. Ask someone if he/she has classes in the morning.
    .
  5. Invite someone to a housewarming.
    .
  6. Tell someone that you work only in the evening.
    .
