Голоса 1, Lesson 5. How would you do the following? (0690g)

Directions: Practice saying your responses out loud in Russian. Pay special attention to the accent. Check your grammar carefully. Reply with complete sentences. You may print or record your responses and hand the exercise to an instructor to be checked for accuracy.
  1. Ask your friend if he/she is going to the university today.
  2. Find out what day is today.
  3. Your friend tells you that he/she is going to the concert. You want to suggest that you go together.
  4. You see your friend on the street and you w nt to find out where he/she is going.
  5. You see your friend in a car and you want to find out where he/she is going.
  6. Find out what time it is.
  7. Your friend wants to chat with you. You want to tell him/her that you have to go.
  8. Find out what your friend is doing on Saturday evening.
  9. Find out when your friend has Russian.
  10. Find out what your friend is doing.
  11. Tell your friend what you are doing on Friday afternoon.
  12. Your friend sees you in your car. Tell him/her where you are going.
  13. Your friend wants to know what time it is. Tell him/her.
  14. Your friend wants to know what time you want to go to the movies. Tell him/her.
  15. Your friend wants to know when you want to go shopping. Tell him/her that you are free all day.
  16. Your friend wants to know when you want to go shopping. Tell him/her on what day you are free.
  17. Your friend wants to know at what time you get up. How would you reply?
  18. Your friend wants to know on what day of the week you clean your room or apartment. How would you reply?
  19. Your friend is inviting you to go to a disco. Tell him/her that you never go to a disco.
  20. How would you start a phone conversation with a friend?
  21. As you are trying to get to know someone, ask them what they usually do on (any day of the week).
  22. Tell your friend at what time you eat lunch.
  23. Tell your friend at what time you go to bed.
  24. Your friend wants to know what you do in the morning. How would you reply?
  25. Ask your friend if he/she goes to [place] every day.
  26. Your friend wants to know where you went yesterday. How would you reply?
  27. Tell your friend where you go often.
  28. Your friend wants to chat. Tell them that you are late for class.
  29. Tell your friend what you have to do today.
  30. Tell you friend what she has to do today.
