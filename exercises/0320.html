Prepare a dialogue according to each template.
Dialogue 1.1

A. --You address?
B. --City, street, house/apartment number.
A. --Thank you.
B. --You're welcome.

Dialogue 1.2

A. --Is this apartment number (number)?
B. --Yes.
A. --Does Name live here?
B. --Yes, here.
A. --And who else lives here?
B. --His parents and his brother/sister Name.

Dialogue 1.3

A. --Is this your brother/sister?
B. --Yes, mine.
A. --And what's his/her name?
B. --Name.
A. --And what's your name?
B. --Name.
