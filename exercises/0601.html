Начало 1, Lesson , Part . How would you say it in Russian? (0601)

Directions: Click on each phrase to hear it pronounced in Russian. Practice saying each phrase in Russian.
1. The neighbor, who lives on the third floor, is very nice.

2. The American woman who lives in our dorm, speaks Russian very well.

3. In our group there are students who are always late.

4. Do you know the girl, who plays the piano so well?

5. Who is this guy, who is opening the door?

6. Where are the books that we bought?

7. The young man, who is parking the car, is our neighbor.
