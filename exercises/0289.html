#0289. мЮВЮКН 1, Lesson 4

In this assignement you will access the home page of a university in Russia and find out as much information about the place as you can without using a dictionary.
  1. Visit the home page of this university
  2. What is the Name of the University?
  3. Click on Руководство. This is a list of the administrators of the university. Who is the Rector of the university? What is his phone number?
  4. Look at the section under Учебные специальности. List the subjects that you can recognize.
  5. Look at the section under Апирантура. What do you think this section refers to? List the subjects that you can recognize.
  6. Go to some of the other sections in the home page and see what else you can find out about the university.
