#0201. Начало 1, Lesson 7, Part 4. How would you do the following?

  1. Tell your mom or dad that you have everything that you need in the apartment.
    .
  2. You want to tell your instructor that he/she is a very good lecturer.
    .
  3. Ask someone what they did last Sunday.
    .
  4. Someone wants to know what your party was like. Tell them that it was lots of fun and name a couple of things that you did.
    .
  5. Name three subjects that you know real well.
    .
  6. Ask someone if he/she likes their math/history/sociology professor.
