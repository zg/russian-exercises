Translation. Начало 1, Lesson 4, Part 1. (#0281)

Directions: Translate each sentence into good Russian. Make several attempts to translate the sentences correctly. After you translate a sentence, click on the Tab key to check your answer. Make several attempts to provide the correct translation. Click on the ? button to see one possible correct translation. Practice saying out loud the Russian sentences.
