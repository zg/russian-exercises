Начало 1, Lesson 3, Part 4. Dialogue templates. (#0278)

Asking about location of places. Prepare a dialogue according to the template.
Places: почта, магазин, автобусная остановка
Location: направо, налево, рядом, далеко, недалеко

A. Do you happen to know where the place is?
B. Here it is, location.
A. And where is the place?
B. The place is location.
A. Is the place near|far?
B. Yes|no, it's near|far.
A. Thank you.
B. You're welcome.
