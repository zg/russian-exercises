#0085. Начало 1, Lesson 3, Сочинение: Давайте познакомимся.

Write a short essay, in which you introduce yourself. Tell your name, where you live (city and street), and two adjectives that describe you the best. Introduce your family members, telling where they live. Use at least one adjective to describe each one. You may also want to write few things about one or more of your neighbors. Start each introduction with Это мой/моя, etc.

Describe your house/apartment and room, and some of the furniture in it. Say few things about your neighborhood. Write about 30 words.
