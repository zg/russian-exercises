Начало 1, Lesson 3, Part 2. Dialogue templates. (#0276)

Directions: Prepare a dialogue according to each template. Practice saying the dialogues without looking at a Russian text. Be prepared to perform the dialogues with a partner.
Dialogue 1

A. Do you work?
B. Yes|No. I am a profession. And do you work?
A. Yes|No. I am a profession

B. Yes|No. I'm a/n profession.
A. Is that a difficul profession?

A. Yes|No, I'm a/n profession.
