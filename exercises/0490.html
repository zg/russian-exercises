Начало 1, Lesson 3, Part 2. How would you do the following? (#0490)

Directions: Practice saying your responses out loud in Russian. Pay special attention to the accent. Check your grammar carefully. Reply with complete sentences. You may print or record your responses and hand the exercise to an instructor to be checked for accuracy.
  1. Ask the people you just met outside if they are your neighbors.
  2. Describe your room to someone using at least two adjectives.
  3. Decribe any two of your relatives using at least two adjectives for each one.

  4. You see a man and a woman walking together. Ask them if they are brother and sister.
  5. Introduce your friend Lena to your mom. Use the appropriate Russian expression for introducing people.
