Начало 1, Lesson 6, Part 3. How would you do the following? (#0161)

  1. Ask your neighbor if the mail came yet.
    .
  2. Ask someone what magazine he/she gets.
    .
  3. Tell someone not to worry.
    .
  4. Ask your friend to whom he/she is writing a letter.
    .
  5. Ask someone where the bus stop is.
    .
  6. Tell someone your phone number.
    .
  7. Ask someone for their phone number.
    .
  8. Someone wants to know who is the man with you. Tell them it's your sister's husband.
    .
  9. Ask someone if they like Mozart's (Моцарт) music.
    .
  10. Ask someone if they know your professor's address.
    .
  11. Ask your mail man to give you your mail.
    .
