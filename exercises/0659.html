Голоса 1, Урок 1. VOCABULARY PUZZLE (0659)

You need Java enabled to view the crossword applet.

If you do not have Java installed you can obtain it from java.com. If you do have Java you may need to check your security settings to make sure that applets are enabled, especially if you are viewing the puzzle from your hard disk. In Windows XP you may be able to enable the applet by clicking on the yellow bar at the top of the window and selecting "Allow blocked content".
To use a letter not on your keyboard click on it beneath the grid.
