Начало 1, Lesson 5, Parts 3-4. How would you do the following? (#0145)

Directions: Practice saying your responses out loud in Russian. Pay special attention to the accent. Check your grammar carefully. Reply with complete sentences. You may print or record your responses and hand the exercise to an instructor to be checked for accuracy.
  1. Tell someone that you can't find/buy a map anywhere.
    .
  2. Tell someone that you can help.
    .
  3. Ask a sales person if they have your size.
    .
  4. Ask your parents if you can buy a car.
    .
  5. Tell someone that you are at home every evening.
    .
  6. Someone wants to sell you something. Tell them it's too expensive.
    .
  7. Ask someone what kind of mood they are in.
    .
  8. Tell someone what kind of a mood you are in.
    .
  9. What is a good 'come on' line to use on a man or a woman?
    .
  10. Find out why someone is in a bad mood.
    .
  11. Ask a friend where you can buy Russian books.
    .
  12. Ask someone if he/she lives alone.
    .
  13. Tell someone that you don't remember their last name.
    .
  14. Tell someone what you do in the evening.
    .
  15. Ask someone if he/she speaks Russian every day.
    .
  16. Ask someone if they have a house or are renting an apartment.
    .
  17. You are stranded and you want to ask someone for help.
